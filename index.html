Airbnb Listing

detailed information for each Airbnb house in terms of price, room types, bed types, reviews and so on.

The basic Airbnb listing view includes the map listing down all the Airbnb houses in London. We use different shapes of symbols to show the different room types like we use door symbol to denote private room. Users can get the density of Airbnb room in different areas of London, their price ranges and room types. They can also use room type pie chart and bar charts to filter by room type or borough area. When users mouse over the symbol on the map, they can see the detailed information of the listing like amenity, hostname, bed types and so on. It gives users an overview on the locations, prices and other details of the Airbnb listings in London.

Amanities

numbers of recorded offences and rates of offences per thousand population by financial year and borough.

The amenity tab aims to provide a guide towards users on how and where to go for the different amenities. In our visualization, we only chose four types of amenities namely bar, arts facilities, nightclub and casinos. This is because when we did online research and text analysis like below we found out these four are the most popular amenities that travelers go for when they visit London.

Crimes Rate

all the different amenities inside London in terms of their location and types.

When users click our crime rate tab, they can see the crime rate in different boroughs using the map visualization. Clearly they can see that Westminster has the highest crime rate per month. If they want to see the distribution of each type of offense in different borough, they can look at the bar chart at the right and see the distribution of crime type. We can see that even though Westminster has the largest crime rate, but majority of it is still theft so travelers do not need to concern so much. Users can also use the pie chart as the filter to filter out the borough they want to see. During a time when terrorists attacks are so prevalent, crime rate or safety in the area should be the primary concern for travelers.
